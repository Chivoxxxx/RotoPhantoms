Team Policies - Roto Phantoms

Team Code of Conduct:

All team members must adhere to the values of teamwork, respect, and collaboration. We aim to build a positive environment where every member’s input is valued. Ethical conduct, punctuality, and dedication are expected at all times.

Meeting Attendance Policy:

Team meetings are mandatory. If a team member is unable to attend a meeting, prior notice must be given to the team leader. It’s important that every member stays up-to-date on team activities and progress.

Equipment Handling:

All team members must follow the guidelines for handling robotics equipment. Mishandling or neglecting the proper care of the equipment may result in disciplinary actions.

Collaboration and Communication:

Team members are expected to communicate openly and professionally. Collaboration tools (e.g., Slack, Google Drive) should be used for project-related discussions and document sharing. All members are encouraged to contribute and share ideas.
